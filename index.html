About Me

I'm an Irish national living in the UK. I started out my data career in Astronomy, where I focussed on exo-planetary systems (i.e. planets around other stars). I have two Masters degrees in Astronomy, one from the University of Groningen, Netherlands, and one from the University of Warwick, UK. Observational Astronomy is a data-rich field, which prepared me well for a career in Data Science. After university, I went to work in the water industry in the UK as the Senior Data Scientist, primarily building machine learning models to detect water leaks, progressing to Head of R&D and Data Analytics where I led a team of Data Scientists and Software Engineers to bring cutting edge ML products to the UK and international water industries. In 2023, I began working at Liberty Global Shared Services Centre as Data Science Manager, responsible for developing end-to-end technical solutions using GCP, educating the business on the capabilities of Data Science and ensuring Data was being leveraged appropriately. Recently, I began working as Data Science Manager at Asda in Leeds, UK.
